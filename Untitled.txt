<p class="about-anthony-header-margin"> Anthony Forrest (born July 25, 1951) played the part of a stormtrooper dazed by Obi-Wan Kenobi in <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0076759/"> Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope</a>. The famous scene takes place at the Mos Eisley roadblock on the planet of Tatooine and Anthony is the Storm Trooper that says "These aren't the droids we're looking for." Antony also played a part of the Fixer in scenes deleted from the movie. There was a documentary made about thr Star Wars characters behind the mask titled <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4558042/">Elstree 1976 (2015)</a>. Below there is an interview with Anthony and Elstree 1976's director John Spira.</p>
                <p>Forrest has worked on over fifty films including: <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0076752/">The Spy Who Loved Me (1977)</a>, <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0074452/">The Eagle Has Landed (1976)</a>, <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0082979/">Reds (1981)</a>, and cult classic <a href="https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0077801/">Killer's Moon (1978)</a>.</p>
                <p>Currently Anthony is script writing, composing music and working as a director.</p>
