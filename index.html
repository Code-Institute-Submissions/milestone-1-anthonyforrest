About Anthony Forrest

Anthony Forrest (born July 25, 1951) played the Fixer in scenes deleted from Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. He also played the part of the stormtrooper dazed by Obi-Wan Kenobi at the Mos Eisley roadblock who said "These aren't the droids we're looking for."

Forrest has worked on over fifty films including: The Spy Who Loved Me, Riders of the Storm and The Eagle Has Landed.

Currently Anthony is script writing, composing music and working as a director on differently upcoming projects.
